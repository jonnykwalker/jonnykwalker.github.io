Identity Access Management

Identity Access Management, at its most basic, focuses on ensuring people or resources have all the access they need, but only the access they need. It touches on all three pillars of the CIA Triad, and is essential to any organization that intends to stay functional, efficient, and secure.

I have worked in IAM at three separate healthcare enterprises and learned from each role. Through my experience, I have seen the importance of the field first hand, whether it is ensuring patients' protected medical records are only seen by qualified and appropriate people or assisting a physician in getting the information and access they need while they are on the floor treating patients.

Scripting and Coding

Identity Access Management is always striving to find ways to automate, to improve the speed of processes, and to reduce human error as much as possible.
